Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

1. Introduction

DENAGO NEV (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website denagonev.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you visit or make a purchase on our site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you contact us, create an account, or place an order, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment details (processed securely by our payment provider)

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We also collect data automatically when you visit our site:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, referral URLs
  • Cookie and analytics data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your account, orders, and updates
  • To improve our website, products, and services
  • To send marketing emails if you have opted in (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site usage and performance
  • Personalize your experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Payment processors for order payments
  • Shipping providers to deliver your orders
  • Marketing platforms if you opt in to receive newsletters
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent where relevant

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@tigongolfcarts.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected information about a minor, please contact us to have it deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent changes. Please review it regularly.
