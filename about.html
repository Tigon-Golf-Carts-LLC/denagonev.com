About DENAGO NEV

Your trusted bridge dealership for DENAGO® neighborhood electric vehicles.

Our Mission

At DENAGO NEV, we partner with Tigon Golf Carts to bring you the full lineup of DENAGO® electric neighborhood vehicles. As a dealership bridge website, we’re dedicated to connecting you with top-quality, sustainable transportation solutions— from ordering to delivery, we streamline your experience every step of the way.

Our Models

  • DENAGO® EV CITY – Compact, agile, and perfect for urban commutes.
  • DENAGO® EV NOMAD – Versatile 4-seater for family and utility tasks.
  • DENAGO® EV NOMAD XL – Extended range and comfort for longer adventures.
  • DENAGO® EV ROVER XL – Rugged 4-seater built for off-road exploration.
  • DENAGO® EV ROVER XL6 – Spacious 6-seater with extra power and cargo capacity.
  • DENAGO® EV ROVER XXL – Heavy-duty 6-seater with forward-facing seats for group outings.

Why Choose DENAGO® NEVs?

DENAGO® neighborhood electric vehicles set the standard for sustainable mobility:

  • Eco-Friendly – Zero emissions and energy-efficient LFP batteries.
  • Performance – Smooth AC motors, reliable disc brakes, and consistent power.
  • Safety – Sturdy chassis, seat belts, weather-rated windshields, and LED lighting.
  • Comfort – Ergonomic seats, ample legroom, intuitive controls, and optional accessories.
  • Warranty – Industry-leading 5-year battery and 2-year parts coverage.
  • Customization – Multiple colors, lift kits, brush guards, and tech add-ons.

Our Dealership Bridge

  • Direct communication with certified DENAGO EV dealers.
  • Post-sale support and service scheduling.

Whether you’re a first-time electric vehicle buyer or adding to your fleet, DENAGO NEV ensures a seamless journey from browsing to delivery.
